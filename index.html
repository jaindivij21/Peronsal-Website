THIS IS MY STORY


Hey there, I am Divij. A budding photgrapher/cinematographer from India. Travel is my drug of choice and i get the zeal of my life by visiting places i have never been to. I continue to explore my journey of purpose. My interest towards nature, moments, people, culture and life motivate me to continue world exploration while capturing unique cultures, people, places and moments around the globe. By profession, i am pursuing engineering in Computer Science but my heart lies in capturing untold stories from around the world. I focus on specializing every genre of photography and continue to learn, explore, master, discover and invent the art of capturing the beauty of Earth.

Travel has been an important part of my life for as long as i can remember. Now more than ever do I realize that I will never be content with a sedentary life, that I will always be haunted by thoughts of a sun-drenched elsewhere. This is the reason i don't want to miss any chance of travelling the world while i meet different people from various fields of life. We travel to bring what little we can, in our ignorance and knowledge, to those parts of the globe whose riches are differently dispersed. And we travel, in essence, to become young fools again- to slow time down and get taken in, and fall in love once more.
